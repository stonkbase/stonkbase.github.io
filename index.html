Synthetic DeFi Stonks


StonkBase is a censorship resistant DeFi protocol for trading synthetic stonks entirely on chain. The synths are similar to cash settled futures, which means they have an expiration date at which they will settle for the underlying collateral. The protocol is based on UMA, whose censorship resistant DVM "oracle" system enables fully decentralized settlement.
